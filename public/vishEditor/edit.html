        1. This is ViSH editor!

          Welcome, this is the application where you can create your excursions. Click on "Next" to continue.

        2. This is the tools menu

          In this bar you can see all the options provided to create and manage a new virtual excursion in ViSH. Click on "Next" to continue.

        3. Configuration Button

          This button will get you to the first screen where you added the title and the description to your excursion. Click on "Next" to continue.

        4. Tutorial buttons

          Clicking these buttons, will get you to every tutorial around the VisH Editor interface

        5. This is the slides menu

          In this zone the slides that you have created will appear. If you click on them you will be moved to that slide and you will be able to edit it. Click on "Next" to continue.

        6. Save Button

          This button will able you to save your excursion.Click on "Next" to continue.

        7. Preview

          This button will get you to preview your excursion as if you were watching it already published

        8. Vish Editor Menu

          This button will allow you to choose some options for the excursion:

          Using this menu you can save your excursion, convert your presentation visualization to a flashcard, preview your excursion before you publish it, change your excursion details and settings and start this tutorial every time that you want to.

          Most of these options are on Vish Editor space, but you can always have them here for an easier use.

        9. Vish Editor Themes

          This button will able you to choose some Visual themes for your presentation, you can change the theme of your excursion every time without saving it.

        10. Toolbar Options

          This space will contain the options to edit your content on the slides:

          • Link Image to Url

          • Resize content

          • Text Editor

          • Flashcard Background

          Every time that you add or select content in your excursion this toolbar will provide you tools to interact with that content.

        11. Add new slide button

          This icon will get you to the templates selection screen to add your slide to your excursion. As you add the slides they will appear at the time-line as an icon providing you with the pagination of it inside the presentation. Click on "Close" to finish this walk through and start adding content to your excursion.

        1. Adding content to a slide

          To fill the slide you chosen with content, you can click the grey spaces in the template.If you click on one of them the content icons will appear:

          • Text

          • Images

          • Object

          • Videos

          • Live

          • Quiz

          If you click one of these icons (depending on which content do you choose to fill the grey space) another window will pop-up to help you, with the exception of the text icon, which will make appear a text toolbar at the top of the application that will help you defining the style for your text.

          Once, you fill the grey space with content, you can edit whenever you want just by clicking on it again, and in case you want to delete the content you can use the icon at the right bottom corner on your space, and if you want to delete a slide you can use the close icon to do the task at the right upper corner:

          • Delete Content

          • Close or Delete

          If you need any help with any of the content you can always use the help icons in their respective windows.

        1. Flashcards

          A flashcard, is an application that will help you represent your excursion as an infographic, using an image to represent the concept of the excursion and mark zones of the image for the user to interact with them in order to see the pages of the excursion.

          First, use these icons to add a background to the slide.

          You can add images by:

          • Url

          • Upload your own images

          • From the vish repository

          • From Flickr

          Once, you finish this step, you can "drag and drop" the arrows in the lower zone of the editor (on top of the slides) into the flashcard background image to mark the zones that you want the user to interact with.

          You can click on the "preview button" to test the flashcard before you save it.

          If you save your presentation as a flashcard, it will be shown on the Vish web page as a flashcard, but you can always edit your excursion back and save it as a presentation.

        1. Virtual Tours

          A virtual tour is a represententation of your excursion as a map.

          Just "drag and drop" the arrows in the lower zone of the editor (on top of the slides) into the map.

          The arrows will be converted into a pin, which you can moved across the map.

          To remove a pin from the map, just click the desired pin.

          You can click on the "preview button" to show the resulting virtual tour before you save it.

        1. Adding a Slide

          This window offers you a variety of templates to create your excursion. You just have to click on one of the image to add it in order to fill it with your content. Click on "Close" to finish this walkthrough.

        1. Insert a PDF presentation

          Select a presentation in PDF format and upload the file. Only files with PDF format are allowed.

        1. Adding Slides from another excursion

          This window offers you to search excursions in the Vish platform in order to add them (or add selected slides from them) into your excursion in just two steps. You just have to click on one of the excusions, click on preview (which will lead you to the next screen) ,choose the slides, and click on "add slides" in order to fill them to your content. Click on "Close" to finish this walkthrough.

        1. Adding Flashcards or Virtual Tours

          This window offers you to search Flashcards or Virtual Tours in the Vish platform in order to add them into your excursion in just two steps. You just have to click on the smartcard you choose, click on preview (which will lead you to the next screen) and click on "add slides" in order to insert it to your content. Click on "Close" to finish this walkthrough.

        1. Vish Editor Themes

          Choose between the different options a theme that fit your excursion, first line of themes are gradients and powerful colours, second line of themes are colourful textures, and the last line of themes are forms that imitates real life objects and one animated theme that will move slowly behind your presentation giving your excursion an special appeal. Have fun!
